CIS Invoice - Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

CIS Invoice is 100% offline. No data collection. No tracking. Your data stays on your device.

Overview

CIS Invoice is designed with your privacy in mind. We believe your business data belongs to you and should stay on your device.

Data Storage

All your data — invoices, clients, expenses, quotes, mileage records, and your business profile — is stored locally on your device only. We do not operate servers that collect or store your information. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share or export it.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal information
  • Invoice or financial data
  • Client information
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics
  • Device identifiers

We have no way to access your data because it only exists on your device.

Sharing & Export

When you use features like "Share PDF", "Email Invoice", or "Export CSV", your device's built-in sharing functionality is used. The data goes directly from your device to your chosen destination (email app, messaging app, etc.). We are not involved in this transfer.

Data Backup

The app provides a backup and restore feature that exports your data as a JSON file to a location you choose on your device. This backup is created and stored entirely under your control. We recommend backing up regularly. If you uninstall the app or reset your device, your data will be lost unless you have created a backup.

In-App Purchases

Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We do not collect or store any payment information. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy or Google's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle purchase data.

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not share any data with third parties.

Children's Privacy

This app is designed for business use by adults. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Your Rights (UK GDPR)

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it at all times. You can view, edit, export, or delete any of your data directly within the app. To delete all data, use the "Clear All Data" option in Settings.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
