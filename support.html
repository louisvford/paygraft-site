Frequently Asked Questions

How do I back up my data?

Go to Settings > Backup & Data > Create Backup. This saves a backup file to your device that you can store in your preferred location (Google Drive, iCloud, email to yourself, etc.). We recommend backing up at least once a week.

What happens if I lose my phone?

All data is stored locally on your device. If you have a backup file, you can restore it on a new device by going to Settings > Backup & Data > Restore Backup. Without a backup, the data cannot be recovered.

How are CIS deductions calculated?

CIS deductions apply only to the labour portion of your invoices. Materials are exempt. The app automatically calculates deductions based on the CIS status you set for each client: 0% (gross), 20% (standard), or 30% (higher rate).

Can I use this app if I'm VAT registered?

Yes. You can enable VAT in your business profile and the app will calculate VAT on your invoices. CIS deductions are calculated on the pre-VAT labour amount, as per HMRC rules.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through the App Store (iOS) or Play Store (Android). Go to your device's subscription settings to manage or cancel. Your data remains on your device even after cancellation.

Is my data secure?

Your data is stored only on your device and is protected by your device's own security (PIN, fingerprint, Face ID). We have no servers and cannot access your data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Can I export my data for my accountant?

Yes. Go to Settings > Export Data to generate CSV files of your invoices, expenses, and CIS summaries. You can also export a full accountant package with everything your accountant needs for Self Assessment.
